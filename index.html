Collection of Surrealism Artwork

This is a collection of my surreal artwork. It's the style I work in the most. These are a mix of digital and traditional; the black-and-white pieces are pen and ink, and the rest were done using Corel Painter, Illustrator, or Photoshop.

Collection of Realism Artwork

Collection of Abstract Artwork

These are a mix of applications and techniques, including Procreate, Photoshop, Corel Painter, and brush-tip markers.

Collection of 3D Multi-Media Artwork

In this section, some of the images are 3D models and some are 3D models that have been painted over. I used Substance Painter, ZBrush, and Blender for the models and texturing. All the faces are completely modelled in ZBrush. The frog monster is painted over in Photoshop, and the silver mask is vectorised and outlined in Illustrator.

Custom Font Project

This was my favourite project in graphic design. We were tasked with creating a font from scratch. I did this using FontLab and Illustrator, and the final product was an installable font that can be used in any software. Beyond the usable font, there were many other deliverables, such as examples of use and a poster displaying the font. The front and back of the poster are on the left, and the examples of use are on the right.
